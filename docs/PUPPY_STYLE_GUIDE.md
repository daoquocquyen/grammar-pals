## Character Vibe
- Friendly, curious, and encouraging.
- Expressive eyes, soft muzzle, rounded ears.
- Never scary or overly realistic; always a plush, sticker-like feel.

## Shape Language
- Big head, small body (chibi proportions).
- Rounded paws and tail.
- Minimal fur details (use color blocks only).

## Expressions (must have)
- **Idle:** calm, gentle smile.
- **Happy:** wide smile, rosy cheeks.
- **Thinking:** raised brow or tiny question bubble.
- **Celebrate:** confetti or sparkles around head.

## Colors
- Base fur: warm cream/peach
- Ear accents: slightly darker peach
- Nose: warm brown
- Cheeks: soft pink (only for happy/celebrate)

## Usage
- Puppy must be visible on gameplay screens at all times (coach bar).
- Puppy is the primary guide; speech lines are short and supportive.
